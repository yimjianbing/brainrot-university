Title: World War I
Description: World War I was a massive global conflict between two main teams, the Allies and the Central Powers, that lasted for years and left deep scars. The war was fought on multiple fronts, including Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and the Asia-Pacific, and saw some major advancements in weaponry, like tanks, planes, and chemical weapons. It was one of the deadliest wars in history, with around 30 million soldiers killed or wounded, and another 8 million civilians dying from war-related causes or genocide. The war also led to a massive movement of people, which helped spread the Spanish flu pandemic
